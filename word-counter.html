Word Count Requirements for Common Writing Tasks

Tweet/X post: 280 characters · Instagram caption: 2,200 chars · LinkedIn post: 3,000 chars · Short blog post: 500–800 words · Standard blog post: 1,000–1,500 words · Long-form SEO article: 1,500–2,500 words · Academic essay: 500–5,000 words · Short story: 1,000–7,500 words · Novel: 70,000–120,000 words.

Reading and Speaking Time Estimates

Average adult reading speed is 200–250 words per minute (wpm) for standard prose. Academic or technical content slows to 100–150 wpm. For presentations, 120–140 wpm is recommended for clarity. At 130 wpm: a 5-min speech needs ~650 words, a 10-min presentation needs ~1,300 words, a 20-min TED talk needs ~2,600 words.
