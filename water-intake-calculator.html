Hydration Tips
  • 🕐
    Spread intake throughout the day. Aim for roughly 1 glass every 1.5 hours across a 12-hour waking period — consistent sipping is more effective than large amounts at once.
  • ☕
    Account for caffeine and alcohol. Both are diuretics. Add approximately 1 extra glass of water for every cup of coffee or alcoholic drink you consume.
  • 🌿
    Count food moisture too. About 20% of daily water intake typically comes from food — fruits, vegetables, soups, and dairy all contribute.
  • 💡
    Check your hydration level. Pale yellow urine generally indicates good hydration. Dark yellow or amber suggests you need more water.
